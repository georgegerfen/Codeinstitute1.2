About Paradise

We are a passionate and dynamic music band that has been creating music for over a decade. Our journey started in the small pubs of the Isle of Wight and we have grown an audience which spans continents.'

With a unique blend of rock, pop, and jazz, we have captured the hearts of music lovers everywhere. Our music is not just a sound; it's a journey, a story, and a feeling that resonates with our listeners.

Join us on this musical adventure, and let our melodies become the soundtrack to your life.
